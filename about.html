Youna (Zhongshan) Commerce & Trade Co., Ltd.

We are a professional foreign trade enterprise dedicated to providing one-stop China sourcing solutions for global buyers. Based in Guangdong — the heart of China's manufacturing hub — we are perfectly positioned to connect international businesses with the best Chinese suppliers.

We specialize in supplying high-quality products across a wide range of categories, including Electronic Products, Toys & Apparel, Household Supplies, Hardware & Tools, Outdoor & Sports Gear, Beauty & Personal Care, Mother & Baby Products, Office Supplies, and Industrial Parts & Components.

Beyond product sourcing, we offer a full range of integrated services to simplify your entire procurement process — from screening and verifying reliable suppliers, to price negotiation, end-to-end quality control, and coordination with trusted logistics partners for customs clearance and international shipping.

By choosing Youna, you can count on on-time delivery and products that fully meet your quality requirements. We take care of all the complexities in China sourcing — so you can focus on growing your core business.
