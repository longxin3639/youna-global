Why Free Warehouse Storage Matters:
  • ✅ Order consolidation: Source from multiple suppliers and consolidate everything in our warehouse
  • ✅ Cost savings: Ship all your products together in one container, not multiple small shipments
  • ✅ Quality control: We inspect everything before it leaves our warehouse
  • ✅ Flexibility: Take your time coordinating payments and logistics

Real Client Example

Last month, a US buyer sourced products from 8 different factories across Zhejiang and Guangdong. Instead of dealing with 8 separate shipments, all products arrived at our warehouse. Pro Tip: Consolidating orders in our warehouse is one of the fastest ways to reduce your total landed cost when importing from multiple Chinese suppliers.
