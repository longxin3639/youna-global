The Problem

Why Amazon Sellers Struggle Sourcing Directly from China

Finding products on Alibaba is easy. Getting them at the right price, right quality, and delivered on time — that's where most sellers fail.

Fake Factories & Scam Suppliers

Many Alibaba listings are from trading companies posing as factories. Without local verification, you risk paying higher prices or getting scammed entirely.

Poor Quality = Bad Reviews

Receiving defective products kills your Amazon listing. Without proper QC inspection before shipment, you're gambling with your seller rating.

Overpaying Without Negotiation

Suppliers quote 20-40% higher prices to foreign buyers. Without a local agent who speaks the language and knows the market, you're leaving money on the table.

Complex FBA Shipping Requirements

Amazon has strict labeling, packaging, and carton requirements. One mistake and your shipment gets rejected at the FBA warehouse.

FAQ

Common Questions from Amazon Sellers

How much does it cost to use a China sourcing agent?
Our fee structure is transparent and straightforward. We charge a service fee based on the order value — there are no hidden commissions from suppliers. Contact us to get a clear quote based on your specific needs.
Can you ship directly to Amazon FBA warehouses in the USA, Germany, or UK?
Yes. We regularly ship to Amazon FBA warehouses across the USA, UK, Germany, Japan, Canada, and other countries. We handle all export documentation, freight forwarding, and FBA compliance labeling.
How do I know the factory you find is legitimate?
We verify every factory by checking their business license, production capacity, and quality records. For higher-value orders, we conduct in-person factory visits. We share verification reports with you before any payment is made.
What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ depends on the product and factory. We work with factories that accept small trial orders for Amazon sellers — often starting from 100-300 units. We'll find options that match your budget and risk tolerance.

Absolutely. We help Amazon sellers create private label products — custom logos, packaging design, product customization, and OEM manufacturing. From prototype to production, we manage the entire process.
