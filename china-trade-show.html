Trade Shows We Cover

Beyond the Canton Fair

The Canton Fair is the biggest, but China has dozens of specialized trade shows throughout the year. We cover the ones that matter for your product category.

Canton Fair (Guangzhou)

The world's largest trade show. Three phases covering electronics, consumer goods, and textiles. Held in April and October.

Yiwu Trade Fair

The world's largest small commodity market. Year-round trading center plus annual import exhibition. Great for general merchandise.

Global Sources (Hong Kong)

Consumer electronics and trade show in Hong Kong. More buyer-friendly environment, stricter exhibitor vetting, popular with Amazon sellers.

Furniture Fairs (Foshan)

Foshan hosts multiple furniture exhibitions throughout the year. Critical for furniture sourcing — many factories are within an hour of these shows.

FAQ

Questions About Trade Show Sourcing

Should I go to the Canton Fair or hire a sourcing agent?
Both have value. Going to the Canton Fair gives you exposure to thousands of suppliers in one place and face-to-face interaction. A sourcing agent gives you verification, negotiation support, and ongoing QC. Many clients do both — visit the fair for discovery, then work with an agent for execution and verification.
How do I prepare for the Canton Fair?
Start 4-6 weeks before: research product categories and potential suppliers, pre-register online at cantonfair.org.cn, book hotels near Pazhou, prepare business cards and sample requests, and create a target list of booths to visit. I can help you research suppliers before you arrive.
How do I tell if a Canton Fair booth is a real factory?
Ask these questions: Can I visit your factory after the fair? What's your MOQ for my brand? Do you export to my country? Can you provide samples? Real factories will answer confidently and invite you to visit. Trading companies often hesitate or deflect.
What's the difference between Phase 1, 2, and 3 at Canton Fair?
Phase 1 (April 15-19): Electronics, machinery, building materials. Phase 2 (April 23-27): Consumer goods, gifts, housewares. Phase 3 (May 1-5): Textiles, office supplies, shoes. Choose your phase based on your product category.
Can I hire someone to visit Canton Fair booths for me?
Yes. I can visit Canton Fair booths on your behalf, photograph products and booths, collect supplier information, ask verification questions, and report back with detailed findings. This is ideal if you can't travel or want to cover more ground than possible alone.
