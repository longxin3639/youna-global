Our Service

What Our Product Sourcing Service Includes

End-to-end sourcing support — from your first inquiry to goods arriving at your door. Every step is managed by our team in Guangdong, China.

01

Understand Your Requirements

We start by learning exactly what you need — product specifications, target price, quantity, quality standards, destination market, and any certifications required. The more detail you give us, the faster we find the right match.

02

Supplier Research & Sourcing

We search across our factory network, 1688, trade databases, and industry connections in Guangdong, Zhejiang, Fujian, and other manufacturing hubs. We compile a shortlist of 3-5 verified factories that match your requirements.

03

Factory Verification & Audit

Before recommending any supplier, we verify their business license, check production capacity, inspect quality management systems, and when necessary, visit the factory in person. We send you photos, videos, and a detailed audit report.

04

Price Negotiation

We negotiate with multiple factories in Chinese to get you the best possible price. We provide a detailed price comparison table so you can see each factory's offer, MOQ, lead time, and payment terms side by side.

05

Sample Management

We coordinate sample production with the selected factory, inspect the sample for quality, and ship it to you for approval. We photograph and document everything — you can review samples remotely with full confidence.

06

Production Monitoring & QC

During production, we track progress and can arrange during-production inspections. Before shipment, we conduct a thorough pre-shipment quality inspection (AQL standard) — checking specifications, function, appearance, and packaging against the approved sample.

07

Shipping & Logistics

We compare freight forwarders in Guangzhou and Shenzhen to find the best shipping rates and transit times for your destination. We handle export documentation, customs clearance coordination, and provide real-time tracking until delivery.

FAQ

Common Questions About Product Sourcing in China

What does a product sourcing agent in China do?
A product sourcing agent helps international buyers find, verify, and negotiate with Chinese manufacturers. The full service typically includes: understanding your product requirements, researching and shortlisting suppliers, verifying factories (business licenses, production capacity, certifications), negotiating prices, arranging samples, monitoring production, conducting quality inspections, and coordinating international shipping. Essentially, we handle everything on the ground in China so you don't have to.
How much does a China sourcing agent charge?
Sourcing agent fees vary by service model. Some charge a commission (typically 3-10% of the order value), while others charge a flat service fee per order. At Youna Global, we provide transparent, upfront pricing with no hidden fees. The savings we negotiate from factory prices almost always exceed our service fee — so using an agent is actually more cost-effective than sourcing on your own.
How do you verify that a factory is legitimate?
We verify factories through multiple channels: checking their Chinese business license (营业执照) and company registration, visiting the factory in person or sending our local team, verifying production capacity and equipment, checking relevant export certifications, reviewing their client history, and cross-referencing with government trade databases. We never recommend a factory that hasn't passed our verification process.
Can you find a specific product I saw on Alibaba or online?
Absolutely. Send us a product link from Alibaba, AliExpress, Amazon, or any website — or just send photos and a description. We'll find the actual manufacturer (not just the trading company listing it) or a comparable factory in China. We often find the exact same factory at significantly better prices because we negotiate directly without platform markups.
What product categories do you source?
We source across virtually all product categories manufactured in China. Our core categories include: electronics and tech accessories, toys and games, clothing and textiles, home and kitchen products, hardware and tools, beauty and personal care, mother and baby products, outdoor and sports equipment, automotive accessories, and packaging/printing. If it's manufactured in China, we can find it.
How long does the sourcing process take?
For most products, we can provide a supplier shortlist within 24-48 hours. Factory verification and price comparison take another 2-3 days. Sample production typically takes 7-15 days. After sample approval, bulk production takes 15-45 days depending on the product and quantity. Shipping takes 5-30 days depending on the method. Total timeline from inquiry to delivery: approximately 4-10 weeks.
Do I need to visit China to work with you?
No. Our entire service is designed to be managed remotely. You communicate with us via WhatsApp, email, or video call. We visit factories on your behalf, take photos and videos, and coordinate everything on the ground. If you do want to visit China in the future, we're happy to arrange factory tours and translation services for you.
