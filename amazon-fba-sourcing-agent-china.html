The Problem

Why Amazon Sellers Lose Money at Chinese Warehouses

You found a great supplier in China. But getting goods Amazon-ready? That's where most sellers fail — costing thousands in rejected shipments and expensive return freight charges.

Wrong FBA Labels

Amazon requires specific FNSKU labels on every unit. Apply them wrong and your entire shipment gets rejected at the warehouse — plus $50+ return fee per pallet.

Non-Compliant Packaging

Poly bag requirements, carton dimensions, weight limits — Amazon's rules are strict. One oversized carton means a rejected shipment and wasted sea freight costs.

Supplier Chaos

Sourcing from 3 different factories means 3 shipments arriving at different times. Without consolidation, you pay 3x shipping costs and juggle multiple tracking numbers.

No QC Before Shipping

Defective goods that leave China can't be returned cheaply. A batch of faulty products at Amazon = negative reviews + inventory removal fees.

FAQ

Common Questions about FBA Prep Service

What is FBA prep and why do I need it?
FBA prep means preparing your products to meet Amazon's strict requirements before they reach the warehouse — proper labeling, packaging, and hazmat compliance. If Amazon rejects your shipment, you pay hefty return fees. Our prep center handles all of this in China before your goods ship.
What are your FBA prep services?
We offer: FBA label application (FNSKU, carton labels), poly bagging, bubble wrapping, SKU labeling, product inspection, weight/dimension verification, hazmat check, and consolidation from multiple suppliers into one shipment.
How long does FBA prep take?
Standard FBA prep takes 3-5 business days after goods arrive at our warehouse. Rush service (1-2 days) is available for urgent shipments. We coordinate with your freight forwarder to sync prep timelines with shipping schedules.
Can you consolidate goods from multiple suppliers?
Yes. This is one of our most popular services. If you're sourcing from 3 different factories across China, we receive all goods at our Guangdong warehouse, QC each batch, apply FBA labels, and consolidate into one shipment — saving you significant shipping costs.
Do you ship to Amazon warehouses in different countries?
We ship to Amazon FBA warehouses in the USA, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Japan, Canada, Australia, and more. We handle all export documentation, customs clearance, and last-mile delivery to the FBA warehouse.
