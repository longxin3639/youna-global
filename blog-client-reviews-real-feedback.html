05 The Packing List That Earned Another "Thank You"

Attention to detail separates good sourcing agents from great ones. One seemingly small step — sending a detailed packing list before shipping — can make a huge difference for importers who need to plan customs clearance, warehousing, and inventory management.

Before shipping out the order, Karsa sent the complete packing list for client confirmation

"Hi! Karsa...thank you so much for your great service again 😊"

Notice the word "again" — this client had worked with us multiple times, and each interaction reinforced their satisfaction.

What a Packing List Includes When You Work With Us

  • 📋 Complete item descriptions with SKU references
  • 📦 Quantities per item, per carton, and total count
  • ⚖️ Gross weight, net weight, and dimensions per carton
  • 🔢 Carton numbering for easy receiving at destination
  • 📸 Photos of the packed cartons before dispatch

This level of detail helps you plan warehouse space, estimate customs duties accurately, and verify shipments upon arrival — all before the goods even leave China.

07 What These Five Stories Have in Common

Looking at these five different conversations across five different clients, some clear patterns emerge about what makes a sourcing relationship successful:

The Five Keys to Building Trust With International Buyers:
  1. Product expertise matters. Clients respond positively when we understand their product requirements deeply — whether it is decorative stones, electronics, textiles, or anything else made in China.
  2. Honesty builds faster than marketing. When we refused to give a random inflated quote and stuck to transparent pricing, it earned more trust than any sales pitch could have.
