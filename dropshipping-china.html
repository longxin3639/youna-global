FAQ

Common Questions About Dropshipping from China

What is a China dropshipping agent?
A China dropshipping agent acts as your local fulfillment partner in China. You sell products on your online store, and when a customer places an order, your agent sources the product, performs quality inspection, packages it, and ships it directly to your customer — all without you holding any inventory. You never touch the product yourself.
How is using a dropshipping agent different from AliExpress dropshipping?
AliExpress dropshipping means buying individual items at retail prices with long shipping times (15-45 days) and no quality control. A dropshipping agent sources directly from factories at wholesale prices (30-60% less), offers faster shipping (5-12 days), performs quality checks, supports custom packaging and inserts, and provides blind shipping — your customers never see the factory name or AliExpress branding.
Do you offer blind dropshipping?
Yes, all our dropshipping shipments are 100% blind. We use plain packaging with only your brand or logo — no factory information, no Chinese invoices, and no supplier details. Your customers will believe the product shipped directly from your own warehouse. We can also add your custom packaging, thank-you cards, and promotional inserts.
What ecommerce platforms do you support?
We work with all major ecommerce platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon FBA/FBM, eBay, Etsy, BigCommerce, Squarespace, Wix, TikTok Shop, and more. We can also integrate with your custom store or accept orders via spreadsheet, email, or direct message. You send orders your way — we handle the rest.
How fast is shipping from China for dropshipping?
We offer multiple shipping options to balance speed and cost. Special logistics lines to the US/EU take 5-12 days and are the most popular choice. ePacket / Yanwen takes 7-15 days and is the most affordable. Express shipping via DHL/FedEx/UPS takes 3-7 days for urgent orders. We recommend special lines for most dropshipping businesses.
What are your fees for dropshipping?
Our fee structure is simple and transparent. We charge a small service fee per order (covering sourcing, QC, and logistics coordination). The product price and shipping cost are quoted upfront for each item — no hidden fees. The savings from factory-direct pricing vs. AliExpress typically more than offset our service fee, so you end up with higher profit margins.
Can I send you products from different suppliers to consolidate?
Absolutely. This is one of our most popular services. If a customer orders items from different suppliers, we can collect them all at our warehouse, pack them together in one package, and ship as a single order. This saves significantly on shipping costs compared to each supplier shipping individually.
