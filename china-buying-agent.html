What We Do

What Does a China Buying Agent Actually Do?

A China buying agent is your local representative on the ground — someone who speaks the language, knows the market, and has established relationships with real factories.

Supplier Search & Verification

We search across factories in Guangdong, Zhejiang, Fujian and beyond. We verify business licenses, production capacity, and product quality before recommending any supplier.

Price Negotiation

We negotiate directly with factory owners in Chinese — getting prices that foreign buyers simply cannot access on their own. Savings of 15-30% are common.

Quality Inspection

We visit the factory and inspect goods before shipment — checking specifications, appearance, function, and packaging. You receive a full photo report.

Warehousing & Consolidation

Store goods from multiple suppliers in our Guangdong warehouse for up to 15-20 days free. Combine everything into one shipment to save on freight.

International Shipping

We compare freight forwarders in Guangzhou and Shenzhen to find the best rates. We handle all customs paperwork and coordinate delivery to your door.

OEM & Custom Branding

Want your own brand? We coordinate custom packaging, logo printing, and OEM manufacturing — from concept to finished product with your brand on it.

FAQ

Common Questions About China Buying Agents

What is the difference between a buying agent and a sourcing agent?
The terms are often used interchangeably. A buying agent focuses on purchasing — finding suppliers and placing orders on your behalf. A sourcing agent has a broader role that may also include market research, product development, and long-term supplier management. At Youna Global, we provide both services as part of a comprehensive procurement solution.
Do I need to travel to China to work with you?
No. Our entire service is designed to be managed remotely. You communicate with us via WhatsApp, email, or video call. We handle everything on the ground in China — factory visits, inspections, and logistics — and keep you updated with photos, videos, and reports.
How do I pay the factory? Is my money safe?
We recommend secure payment methods such as T/T (bank transfer) with a deposit structure — typically 30% upfront and 70% after passing inspection. We never handle your payment to the factory directly; you pay the factory according to agreed terms, and we ensure the order is fulfilled correctly.
Can you help me find a specific product I saw online?
Yes. Send us a product link, photo, or description — and we'll find the manufacturer or a similar factory in China. We source across all major product categories including electronics, home goods, toys, clothing, hardware, and more.
How long does the sourcing process take?
We typically respond with a supplier shortlist within 24-48 hours. Sample production takes 7-15 days. After sample approval, bulk production time varies by product (usually 20-45 days). Shipping takes 5-30 days depending on the method and destination.
