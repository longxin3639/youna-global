FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about sourcing World Cup merchandise from Yiwu

What is the minimum order quantity for custom World Cup products?
MOQs in Yiwu start from as low as 50–100 pieces for most categories. Custom printed jerseys typically require 100 pcs minimum, standard fan gear like flags or foam fingers starts from 200 pcs, and promotional items can start from 50 pcs.
How long does production take?
Standard in-stock products can be ready in 3–7 days. Custom printed or embroidered items require 15–25 days. LED or electronic items such as smart wristbands may need 20–30 days. We'll confirm the exact timeline once your order specs are finalized.
Can you help with FIFA licensing and IP compliance?
We advise on IP-safe sourcing options — generic football-themed merchandise (blank jerseys, generic mascots, footballs without official logos) is generally safe. For officially licensed FIFA products, you would need to work with an authorized FIFA licensee. We help you avoid products that infringe on protected trademarks.
What shipping options are available from Yiwu?
We offer sea freight (20–35 days, most cost-effective for bulk), air freight (5–10 days, higher cost per kg), and rail (15–20 days to Europe, a good middle ground). We'll recommend the best option based on your volume, budget, and timeline.
Do you offer quality inspection before shipping?
Yes — quality inspection is a core part of our service. We conduct on-site inspections at raw material stage, during production, and on finished goods before they leave the factory. You receive detailed photo and video reports at each stage.
Can I combine World Cup products with other orders in your warehouse?
Absolutely. Our free warehousing (15–20 days) allows you to consolidate World Cup merchandise with other orders from different suppliers into a single shipment. This typically reduces freight costs by 20–40% compared to shipping each order separately.
