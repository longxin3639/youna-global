Why It Matters

What Happens Without Quality Control?

Skipping inspection is one of the most common and costly mistakes when importing from China.

Wrong Products Shipped

Suppliers sometimes send substitutes or mix products. Without inspection, you won't discover this until the goods arrive at your warehouse — thousands of miles away.

High Customer Refund Rates

Defective products lead to bad reviews, returns, and account suspension on Amazon or other platforms. Your brand reputation is at risk every time a poor-quality product ships.

Money Lost, Hard to Recover

Once products are shipped and customs cleared, getting compensation from a Chinese supplier is extremely difficult. Prevent the problem before it leaves the factory.

Inspection Scope

What We Check During Inspection

Every inspection follows a structured checklist tailored to your product type. Here's what our QC team covers on-site at the factory.

Quantity Verification — Count total units against your purchase order. Detect shortages or overages before shipment.
Appearance & Workmanship — Check for scratches, dents, color inconsistencies, poor stitching, sharp edges, or any visible defects.
Dimensions & Weight — Measure key dimensions and weight against approved specifications and tech pack.
Function Testing — Power on electronics, test zippers, locks, switches, or any mechanical parts that must operate correctly.
Packaging Inspection — Verify inner carton, outer carton, barcode labels, country of origin marking, and drop-test packaging durability.
Branding & Labels — Confirm logo, model number, certifications (CE, FCC, RoHS), and all printed information match your requirements.

Service Types

Types of QC Inspection We Offer

Pre-Production Inspection

We visit the factory before production starts to verify raw materials, samples, and production capability. Catch problems before any units are made.

Prevent Problems Early

During Production Inspection (DUPRO)

Inspect when 20–30% of production is completed. Identify issues early enough for the factory to correct remaining units before it's too late.

Mid-Process Check

Pre-Shipment Inspection (PSI)

The most popular option. We inspect when 80–100% of production is complete and packed, right before the goods are loaded for shipping. The last chance to stop defects.

Most Popular

FAQ

Common Questions About QC Inspection

How much does an inspection cost?

Pricing depends on the product type, factory location in China, and inspection scope. Contact us for a free quote — most pre-shipment inspections are very affordable compared to the cost of a bad batch.

How long does an inspection take?

A standard pre-shipment inspection takes 1 day at the factory. You'll receive the full written report with photos within 24 hours of the inspection date.

What if the products fail inspection?

We provide a detailed failure report with photos. You can then negotiate with the supplier to have defects fixed, request a replacement production run, or refuse shipment. We help you communicate with the factory.

Do you inspect at any factory in China?

We primarily cover Guangdong province (Foshan, Dongguan, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Zhongshan) and can arrange inspections across China including Zhejiang, Fujian, and Jiangsu. Tell us your factory location and we'll confirm coverage.

Can you do inspection if I don't use you for sourcing?

Absolutely. You can hire us for inspection only, even if you already have a supplier. Just send us the factory address, contact details, and your PO specifications.
