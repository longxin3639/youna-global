FAQ

Common Questions

How fast can you find a product for me?

In most cases, we confirm factory details, compare prices, and share full information within 24 hours of receiving your inquiry.

Do you charge for product sourcing?

Initial inquiries are free. Our fees depend on the scope of service — we'll be fully transparent about any costs before proceeding.

Can you help with small orders?

Yes! We work with both small businesses and large importers. There's no minimum order requirement to start a conversation with us.

How do you verify that a factory is legitimate?

We check business licenses, visit factories in person where possible, and share photos and videos with you before any order is placed.

What products can you source?

We cover 9 major categories: Electronics, Toys & Apparel, Household Supplies, Hardware & Tools, Outdoor & Sports Gear, Beauty & Personal Care, Mother & Baby Products, Office Supplies, and Industrial Parts. If it's made in China, we can source it.

What payment methods do factories accept?

Most accept T/T (bank transfer). Some also accept PayPal, Alipay, or Western Union. We'll advise on the safest payment method for your specific order.
